Guide to Rajmachi Trek

Created with First Hand Experience

Posted on Jan 07, 2019

If you are planning to hike rajmachi then read the following guide prepared based on first hand experience.

How to reach Rajmachi Via Lonavala ?

If you are coming from Pune or Mumbai then you can reach rajmachi from Lonavala. To avoid getting lost it is highly recommended you to have google map with good Internet connection or offline copy of google map in your GPS enabled mobile phone.

By Car

You can take Mumbai Pune Highway (NH48) and reach base village Udhewadi which is near from Lonavala. Udhewadi to Rajmachi village road is not in very good condition to drive vehicle especially in Moonsoon.

During monsoon road will be very muddy and you can at max take your vehicle till Jambhali phata. Villagers block road at Jambhali phata by digging road during moonsoon to prevent vehicles from getting in. Car parking is not available at Jambhali phata so you will have to park on your own risk.

However during non monsoon season car can go up till rajmachi village (Udhewadi) but it will be bumpy ride for sure. Good thing is paid car parking is available at rajmachi village.

By Walking

Get down at Lonavala railway station and head towards NH48 to reach Tungarli Lake. From Tungarli lake till base village udhewadi is a straight road. Total distance from Lonavala station till base village Udhewadi is around 15 KM. So make sure you have prepared itinerary accordingly.

What to bring with you ?

Below we have preparered short checklist of things that you should bring. Though list is not complete but will take care of essential things.

  • 2 liter of Water Bottle
  • Light Snacks - Cakes, Chips, Biscuit etc
  • Energy Drinks such as Glucon D, Soft Drinks and Juices
  • Charged Light Torch with good backup.(at least 6 Hrs)
  • Charged Powerbank
  • 2 Set of Cloths and Towel (Non Moonsoon)
  • 3 Set of Cloths and Towels (Moonsoon)
  • Trekking Shoes with Nice Rubber Grip (Must in Moonsoon)
  • Wincheater and Bagcover (Must in Moonsoon)
  • Extra Pair of Socks
  • Hand Sanitizer
  • First aid and other essential medicines.
  • For Stay
    • Mosquito Repellent
    • Soaps, Mouthwash Blanket etc

FAQs

Difficulty Level Of Trek

Trek is very simple but needs some endurance if you are walking from Lonavala. Road tlll rajmachi can be muddy and slippery but not very difficult for beginners. Need some caution during exploration of fort as rocks can become slippery during monsoon.

Are there Waterfalls ?

There are small waterfalls during trail but none to have bath or to get completely drench in.

Arrangement for the Breakfast, Lunch, Stay and Cost of each ?

Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner and Stay is available at rajmachi village provided by local villagers. Cost would vary depending on services they are offering. Below is the average cost as of 2018.

Is Stay Essential ?

If you are completing whole trek by walking then stay will be required to refuel yourself. If you have enough endurance then stay is not necessary.

Bathroom and Toilets ?

Toilets are available at rajmachi village, only if you are opting for stay.

Vehicle Rides Services ?

During monsoon sumo vehicle ride from Jambhali Phata till Lonavala and other way around is available. Cost would be Rs 1000 per ride. (6 People). You can talk with locals at Rajmachi and ask them to arrange car in advance.
